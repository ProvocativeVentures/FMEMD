Overview

We integrate advanced analytics and public data to develop a predictive model based on the menstrual cycles of female executives. Our unique approach provides insights into market trends influenced by natural physiological rhythms.

Strategy

Our system continuously adjusts its trading strategy—including buying, selling, and short-selling—based on predictive cycle insights. This dynamic model allows us to respond rapidly to market fluctuations.

Cycle Phases & Performance

Menstrual Phase

Characterized by lower energy and introspection, this phase may lead to conservative decisions with a focus on maintaining stability in trading.

Follicular Phase

Rising estrogen drives creativity and optimism, unlocking opportunities for proactive investments and market expansion.

Ovulatory Phase

At peak estrogen, enhanced cognitive function and assertiveness drive high-impact trades that capture market momentum.

Luteal Phase

A shift toward risk aversion and consolidation prompts a defensive approach, focusing on securing existing positions.
